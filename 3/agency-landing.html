Supporting Open Source Blockchain Technology

The æternity Foundation is a non-profit organization established in the Principality of Liechtenstein. The Foundation brings together all the necessary resources and infrastructure to ensure a stable environment and thriving open source ecosystem around the æternity blockchain. The organization is committed to promoting technical excellence and user-friendly applications as well as supporting projects built on the æternity blockchain.

Supported Projects

The Aeternity Foundation exists at the intersection of technology and philanthropy; a think tank for the digital future and a force for good, leveraging the efficiency and transparency of cryptocurrency with real projects and real results. Ecosystem Grant Program

To succeed long term, the æternity Foundation is dedicated to enable community-driven innovation to benefit people around the world, and sustaining open source blockchain technology and its development.

The Foundation invites individuals, teams and non-commercial projects to apply for a grant or donation.
